Hello, Redstone.
We should talk.

I'm Evin. Designer, Developer, Animator.

I am a front-end developer and user interface designer. I love creating sleek, responsive websites that users enjoy interacting with. I could spend all day in the depths of well-organized HTML, complex CSS, and judicious JavaScript. With so many amazing animation tools and libraries out there, I could never learn all there is to know, but that certainly won't stop me from trying.

Let's Talk About Me

Who I Am

Passionate, focused, dedicated, and fun-loving. Let's be honest, these adjectives sound like a cliche at this point, but without these qualities, I wouldn't be where I am today. I'm passionate about what I do, allowing me to pack up my life and move away from the home I knew, in order to chase the career I wanted. Through the many challenges, I focused on what was important: becoming a great developer. Dedication allowed me to follow through

What I Love

Life is so full of interesting, exhilarating, and beautiful things to see and do. It's hard to choose what we want experience within one lifetime! I've been very lucky to have discovered so many of my passions in life already.

Day-to-day, I love jamming out to all kinds of music, singing loudly in my car, and coming up with new concepts for video games. After designing, drawing, and coding, my deepest passion is horseback riding. The greatest experience I've had in my life is horseback riding in the ocean in Jamaica. Yes, horses are great swimmers, and they don't mind having passengers! One day I would like to own a horse again, and become a great show jumper.

Why I'm Here

Born and raised in Michigan, I came to Omaha to start my career as a developer. After landing in a video game development start-up, I fell in love with this city, and the people in it. I soon found my calling in web development, which allows me to combine my passion for art, design, and an appreciation for clean code.
